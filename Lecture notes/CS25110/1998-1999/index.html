  • Practicals:
      The pracs will be held in the DSL Lab. You will work in pairs, and in total there are three experiments to do. Each experiment will take one 2 hour prac slot (probably on alternate weeks) and you will be expected to write up each experiment. Each student will thus spend a total of 6 hours in the lab plus some time producing the report accounting in total for 10% of the marks for this course (10 credits = nominally 80 hours work). This work will be handed in near the end of the semester. The pracs will not start until 3-4 weeks in to the course.

      The report for the CS25110 practicals should be handed in between 2.30-3.30pm on friday 7th May. The style should be that of a scientific report. This implies it will be a clear, concise description of what was done, with a level of detail such that a reader of the report would be able to reproduce the experiment. You should include a summary of what was done and what was learned together any results (graphs etc), observations or analysis you made. I would expect the write up of each experiment to require a maximum of 2-3 pages.
