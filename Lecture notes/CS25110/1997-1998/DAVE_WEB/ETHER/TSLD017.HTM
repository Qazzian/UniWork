Frame details:

  • Minimum frame size 512 bits not including the preamble. ie. 46 octets of data.
  • Maximum frame size 1518 octets. ie. 1500 octets of data.
  • Above assumes 48 bit (6 octet) addresses which IEEE 802.3 says “shall be used” for 10 Mbps networks.
  • Also 9.6 micro second interframe gap.
  • Time for frame is 51.2 micro seconds plus 6.4 micro seconds for preamble.
