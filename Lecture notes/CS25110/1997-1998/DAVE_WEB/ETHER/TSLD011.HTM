Other Medium Options (IEEE 802.3)

  • Original (10BASE5)
    • 10Mbps using baseband coaxial cable, segment length 500m, max. 100 taps per segment, max. 4 repeaters between any pair of stations.
  • 10BASE2 (issued 1985)
    • thinner coaxial cable, segment length 185m, max. 30 taps per segment.
  • 1BASE5 (StarLAN)
    • unshielded twisted pair, 1Mbps, passive star topology, aimed at PC users.
  • 10BASE-T
    • similar to 1BASE5 but at 10Mbps.
