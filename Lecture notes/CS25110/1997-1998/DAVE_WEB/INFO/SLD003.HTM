note that independent is a term from statistics, for our purposes simply think of it as meaning there is no relationship between one event and the next.

e.g. events “raining” and “put up umbrella” may be correlated so given information that one has occurred (i.e. it is raining) may affect our ability to predict the second.

the “b” in the formula determines unit of information, i.e. b=2 provides “bits” worth of information
