Bandwidth

  • “How big is the pipe ?”
  • Expressed in Hz
  • Depends on the type of medium:
    • twisted pair Khz (Mhz for short distances)
    • coaxial cable 100Mhz+
    • fibre Gbit/s
    • microwave
    • etc
  • nHz = n complete oscillations per second
