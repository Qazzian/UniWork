Analogue v Digital Channel

Analogue v Digital Channel

  • Analogue
    • Designed to carry analogue signals. Often designed for voice traffic. Digital data needs to be converted to be carried by the network. A MODEM, modulator/demodulator, performs the conversion.
  • Digital
    • Designed to carry digital information. Digital data traffic is carried easily but analogue traffic needs to be converted using a coder/decoder (CODEC).
