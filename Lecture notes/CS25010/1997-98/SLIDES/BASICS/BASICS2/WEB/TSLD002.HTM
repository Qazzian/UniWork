Publicly Available Networks

  • PSTN - Public Switched Telephone Network
    • Originally full analogue dial-up network for voice
  • PSS (GNS) - Public Data Networks
    • packet switched data network (circuit networks also exist)
  • Leased Lines
    • hired 24 hours/day - analogue and digital
  • ISDN - Integrated Digital Services Network
    • fully digitally dial-up network
