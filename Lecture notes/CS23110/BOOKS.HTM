CS23110 Small Real-Time Systems

Textbook information

You are not required to purchase a textbook for this course. You will probably find it sufficient to consult books from this list in the library, as required.

J. E. Cooling, "Software Design for Real Time Systems", International Thomson Computer Press, 1991. ISBN1-85032-279-1.
Note that this book and its later reprints are out of print, but it is still the best I have found. Consulting it via the library should be sufficient.

David Budgen, "Software Design", Addison Wesley, 1996. ISBN 0-201-54403-2. Publisher's information is available on-line.

Alan Burns & Andy Wellings, "Real-Time Systems and their Programming Languages", Addison Wesley. Second Edition: 1997. ISBN 0-201-40365-X. Publisher's information is available on-line.

See also the booklist attached to the formal syllabus for this module but note that the information on this page is more up to date.
