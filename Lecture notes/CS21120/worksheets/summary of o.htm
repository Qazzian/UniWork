Suppose you have a O(N) algorithm and you double the N

-the algorithm will be twice as long

 

Suppose you have a O(N^2) algorithm and you double the N

-the algorithm will be 4 times as long since (2N)^2 / N^2 = 4

 

Suppose you have a O(log N) algorithm and you double the N

-        the algorithm will increase from c*logN to c*log (2N) or c*(1 +log N) or just a constant number larger
