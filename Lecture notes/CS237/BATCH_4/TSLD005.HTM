& and * operators

The & operator allows us to get the address

of a variable, I.e. gets us a pointer to a data area.

Conversely, the * operator lets us get at the data

pointed to by a pointer.

main()

{ int x, y, * intptr;

x = 5;

intptr = &x;

y = * intptr;

}
