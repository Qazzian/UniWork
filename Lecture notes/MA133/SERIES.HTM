Sequences and Series
In computer science summations occur when trying to find the number of operations performed by the execution of a program. This is important when trying to analyze the efficiency of a particular algorithm. Formulae exist for the summation of certain types of series while for others a guess of the closed form solution may be attempted and then proved using mathematical induction.
