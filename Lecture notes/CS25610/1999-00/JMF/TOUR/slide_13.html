Most methods available on a player are only available in certain states and cause exceptions to be raised in invoked while the player is in an inappropriate state.

Events are raised as players move between states. Most methods that request a player to move between states act asynchronously. That is, the method call will return before the player has changed state. One must wait for events to know that a state transition has occured.
