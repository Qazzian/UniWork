JMF Evolution

  • JMF 1.0 - provided facilities for programmers to develop applications and applets that presented time-based media
  • JMF 2.0 - extends the framework to provide
    • capturing and storing of media data
    • control of processing during playback
    • custom processing on media streams
    • a Plug-In API to permit customization and extension of JMF functionality
